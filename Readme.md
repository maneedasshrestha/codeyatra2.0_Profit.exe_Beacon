# Beacon

College is hard enough without having to hunt down reliable notes, overpay for textbooks, or feel like you're missing out on what's actually happening on campus.

**Beacon** is built by students, for students. It's a central hub for everything you actually need to survive and thrive: a shared vault for community-vetted notes, a marketplace to trade gear with people you trust, and a community feed to keep you in the loop. It's your digital campus companion—one that's always got your back.

---

## What Beacon Does

### Academic Resource Vault
Stop digging through disorganized group chats for PDFs. Access a community-curated library of notes and study materials. Our community-driven voting system ensures that the best resources are easy to find, while dynamic previews let you check content without downloading dozens of files.

### Campus Marketplace
Textbooks and lab equipment shouldn't break the bank. Beacon provides a dedicated space to buy, sell, or trade essentials directly with your peers. It’s local, it’s safer, and it keeps resources within the student community.

### Community Feed
Stay connected with what's happening on the ground. The real-time feed is where you share updates, ask questions, and get the pulse of the campus. Like, comment, and build a network that actually matters.

### AI Student Advisor
Need quick advice from someone who's been there? Our AI assistant adopts a "Senior Student" persona to help with academic queries, project roadblocks, or navigating college life. Powered by Google Gemini, it's like having a mentor available 24/7.

## Tech Stack

### Frontend
- **Framework**: [Next.js 16](https://nextjs.org/) (React 19)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS 4](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Animations**: [Framer Motion](https://www.framer.com/motion/)
- **Icons**: [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)

### Backend
- **Server**: [Express.js](https://expressjs.com/)
- **Database & Auth**: [Supabase](https://supabase.com/)
- **AI Engine**: [Google Gemini AI](https://deepmind.google/technologies/gemini/)

## Why Beacon?
We built Beacon to solve the real frustrations of student life: fragmented information, high costs of academic materials, and the need for a stronger digital community that translates into real-world campus connections.
